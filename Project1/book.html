The True Lives of the Fabulous Killjoys

by Gerard Way, Shaun Simon

RM 54.90

A graphic novel about rebel figures called the Fabulous Killjoys fighting a futuristic corporate dictatorship, located in Battery City. The story centers on the group's fight against the oppressive Better Living Industries, with the comic following The Girl, a character that seeks a new generation of Killjoys to continue the resistance.
